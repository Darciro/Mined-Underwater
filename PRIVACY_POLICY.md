# Privacy Policy for Mined Underwater

**Last Updated:** January 14, 2026

## Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Mined Underwater ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use Mined Underwater, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Data**: Game progress, gameplay statistics, in-game interactions, and session duration
- **Performance Data**: Crash logs, diagnostic information, and performance metrics

### Information You Provide

- **Optional Account Information**: If you choose to create an account or sign in through third-party services
- **Support Communications**: Information you provide when contacting us for support

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Game Functionality**: To provide and maintain the game experience
- **Personalization**: To personalize your gaming experience and save your progress
- **Improvements**: To analyze usage patterns and improve game performance
- **Technical Support**: To respond to your requests and provide customer support
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

## Third-Party Services

Mined Underwater uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

### Advertising Services

We use advertising networks to display ads within the app. These services may collect:
- Device advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Usage data and ad interaction information

**Ad Networks Used:**
- IronSource/Unity LevelPlay
- Other ad mediation partners

For more information about how these services handle your data:
- [IronSource Privacy Policy](https://developers.is.com/ironsource-mobile/air/ironsource-mobile-privacy-policy/)
- [Unity Privacy Policy](https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy)

### Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with our app. These services collect:
- Session information
- Device and usage statistics
- Performance metrics

## Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the game services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Game progress and settings may be stored locally on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Mined Underwater does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete such information.
